Brand Identity & System Application

Conceptual Rebrand: Aura Home Goods

The Challenge: To develop a minimalist and sophisticated brand identity for a new line of sustainable home textiles. The identity needed to feel premium and convey trustworthiness in sourcing without being overly clinical.

My Solution: I designed a geometric, thin-line logomark that suggests weaving and connection. The color palette of muted earth tones (terracotta, beige, taupe) provides a warm, authentic aesthetic that translates cleanly across digital and physical assets like stationery and canvas totes.

Keywords: Logo Design, Typography, System Application, Conceptual Project.

Packaging Design & 3D Mockup

Conceptual Packaging: Summit Hard Seltzer

The Challenge: To create disruptive and visually bold packaging for a premium hard seltzer brand entering a saturated market. The design needed to evoke a sense of rugged, high-quality flavor while maintaining shelf visibility.

My Solution: I utilized a dark-mode approach with a matte black can to establish a high-end feel. The contrast is achieved using a bright cyan and white palette, with topographical map lines overlaid to emphasize the "Summit" concept. This contrast ensures maximum visual punch.

Keywords: Packaging, 3D Rendering, Consumer Goods, Typography.

Corporate Identity & Logo Design

Conceptual Branding: Ascend Financial Group

The Challenge: To design a timeless and aspirational logo for a high-end financial services firm. The mark needed to convey stability, growth, and professionalism, suitable for both digital use and high-quality print.

My Solution: I developed a sleek, metallic 3D wordmark paired with an abstract bar graph motif. The upward-curving arrow symbolizes consistent growth, while the minimal sans-serif typography maintains a modern, authoritative corporate aesthetic.

Keywords: Logo Design, Corporate Branding, Typography, Metallic Mockup.

Commercial Marketing & Call-to-Action

Commercial Banner: Haruna's Shawarma!

The Challenge: To design a high-impact, attention-grabbing banner for local advertising that clearly emphasizes the product quality and provides essential purchase information in a concise format.

My Solution: I used a bright, warm color palette (yellow) for high contrast and visual energy. The layout pairs appetizing, high-quality food photography with clear, bold typography to ensure the address and phone number are immediately visible and actionable.

Keywords: Commercial Layout, Food Photography Integration, Visual Hierarchy, Marketing.

Cultural Identity & Heritage Branding

Conceptual Brand: TRADITION Apparel

The Challenge: To create a brand mark for a conceptual apparel line that celebrates and modernizes African cultural heritage. The design needed to be respectful, visually rich, and instantly communicate pride and quality.

My Solution: I centered the design on a powerful circular composition featuring illustrated figures wearing traditional attire, symbolizing unity and roots. The arched typography uses a refined serif font to convey sophistication and historical value.

Keywords: Cultural Design, Typography, Illustration, Brand Storytelling.

Iconography & Digital Service Identity

Abstract Icon: Digital Streaming Service

The Challenge: To design a dynamic, recognizable logomark for a modern streaming or audio service that suggests both listening (waves) and playback (the play button), optimized for small digital screens.

My Solution: I used abstract, fluid shapes and a vibrant, split-gradient color palette (teal and orange) to create visual energy. The centralized play button integrated into the abstract head form ensures the mark is immediately understood as media-related.

Keywords: Icon Design, Digital Branding, Gradients, Abstract Form.
